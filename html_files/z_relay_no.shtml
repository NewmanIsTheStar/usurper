Relay Normally Open vs. Normally Closed

  • Normally Open (NO) realy contacts must be used to control the irrigation valves.
  • It is not safe to use Normally Closed (NC) as this will result in all irrigation values attempting to open if power is lost to the controller.
  • Attempting to open all the valves at once could result in an overcurrent situation for your 24 VAC power supply.
